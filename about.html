Burlington, Vermont

A small house for small producers.

Ten years of curating certified-organic food, wellness, and natural care from the people who actually make it.

GreenKart began with a station wagon, six regular customers, and a conviction that most organic eCommerce was putting the wrong word first. The word that mattered to us — still — is small. Small producers. Small batches. Short distances.

Ten years on there are fourteen thousand of you, one hundred and twenty producers across four continents, and a team of twenty-two who have, at one point or another, all driven the station wagon.

Our standards are straightforward. Every producer in our network holds an independent organic or equivalent certification. Nothing we sell travels further than it has to. We take ten percent of the sale price; the producer takes the rest.

We still answer the phone ourselves. It rings less than you might expect, and we like it that way — it usually means the boxes are arriving on time.

A short history, honestly told.

2016

A station wagon and six customers.

Our first delivery route ran three farms, six houses, and a Sunday morning. The cooler went in the passenger seat.

2020

Twelve producers, one thousand subscribers.

Moved into a converted dairy barn on Maple Grove Road and published our first seasonal journal.

2023

B-Corp certification.

Independent audit confirmed fair wage, closed-loop packaging, and a carbon-neutral delivery network.

2026

120 producers, 24 states.

Volume Twelve of the seasonal journal goes out on the first Saturday of April — the day you are, probably, reading this.
